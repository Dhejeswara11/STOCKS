About Stock Management System

The Stock Management System is a web-based application developed using Python Django. It is designed to help businesses efficiently manage their inventory, track stock levels, manage suppliers and customers, and monitor sales and purchases in real time.

This system provides a user-friendly interface, allowing administrators to:

  • Track inventory across multiple categories
  • Manage supplier and customer information
  • Generate reports for stock in/out activities
  • Receive low-stock alerts and reorder notifications
  • Ensure accurate inventory records and reduce human error

Our goal is to simplify inventory control and provide a scalable, reliable, and secure solution for businesses of all sizes.

Developed with ❤️ using Django & Bootstrap.
